Accessibility

Contrast and focus styles are included. Use semantic HTML and alt text for images; ensure form controls are labeled.

User stories (selected)

  • Signup & Login — secure authentication (signup/login/logout).
  • Seller Profile — create/edit seller profile with photo and bio.
  • Product Listing — add products with photos, pricing, edit/delete.
  • Product Browsing — responsive grid, product cards, pagination or lazy-load.
  • Search & Filter — by category, price, seller.
  • Checkout (mocked) — simulated payment flow for testing.

Work items / next steps
